# Bank

[

](#articleComments)[Sign in to edit](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fhardware-tycoon.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FBank%3Fveaction%3Dedit&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article)

-   [History](/wiki/Bank?action=history)
-   [Purge](/wiki/Bank?action=purge)
-   [Talk (0)](/wiki/Talk:Bank?action=edit&redlink=1)

The **bank** is accessible at all times. It has got three tabs: loans, taxes, and other.

## Loans\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fhardware-tycoon.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FBank%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")\]

[![The loans tab as of 0.2](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/hardware-tycoon/images/f/fc/0.2.9_Bank.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/184?cb=20210211195037)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/hardware-tycoon/images/f/fc/0.2.9_Bank.png/revision/latest?cb=20210211195037)

[](/wiki/File:0.2.9_Bank.png)

The loans tab as of 0.2.9

You can take and repay loans in the Loans tab.

At the beginning of the game the maximum loan you can take is $500,000. It increases by a fixed amount depending on the [difficulty](/wiki/Game_Settings#Difficulty_Settings "Game Settings") setting every year.

The interest rate, deducted every month, is set at 2% and does not count as loan repayment.

## Taxes\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fhardware-tycoon.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FBank%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D2&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")\]

[![The taxes tab as of 0.2](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/hardware-tycoon/images/1/1e/0.2.10_bank_taxes_tab.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/184?cb=20210730185318)](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/hardware-tycoon/images/1/1e/0.2.10_bank_taxes_tab.png/revision/latest?cb=20210730185318)

[](/wiki/File:0.2.10_bank_taxes_tab.png)

The taxes tab as of 0.2.10

You can view how much [tax](/wiki/Tax "Tax") you will have to pay next year based on your current balance or pay it manually if the *Automatically pay tax on the first day of the year* checkbox is unchecked.

In the event of failure to pay tax before the 3rd month of the year, a fine in the amount of 33% of the due tax on top of that tax is automatically paid.

## Other\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fhardware-tycoon.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FBank%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D3&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")\]

Currently the only purpose of this tab is to give the ability to repay bailouts.
