[](/wiki/File:0.2.8_Finances.png)

The finances tab as of 0.2.8

The finances tab is accessible by clicking on the money display in the top-right corner.

It allows you to see a chart of your balance over time, annual expenses and income, and a finance log with all your spendings & earnings logged.
