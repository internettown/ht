WARNING: This page is incomplete & only shows minimal basic details about this feature. There may or may not be false info here so don't use this stub as a reliable source for now!

Popularity is influenced by the sales of your CPU, as well as marketing. It is not known what popularity does, but it may or may not apply a multiplier on your CPU sales - as that is what popularity does. Marketing may or may not also increase your popularity, as advertising makes it so that more people see your ad > more sales!
