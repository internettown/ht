**Milestones** help to illustrate your company's growth; they work like achievements.

## Company milestones\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fhardware-tycoon.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FMilestones%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")\]
