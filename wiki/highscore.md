# High score

[

](#articleComments)[Sign in to edit](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fhardware-tycoon.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FHigh_score%3Fveaction%3Dedit&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article)

-   [History](/wiki/High_score?action=history)
-   [Purge](/wiki/High_score?action=purge)
-   [Talk (0)](/wiki/Talk:High_score?action=edit&redlink=1)

The role of **high scores** is to introduce replayability and differentiate between weak and outstanding playthroughs. They were added in version [0.2.8.1](/wiki/Changelog#0.2.8.1 "Changelog").

High scores get automatically counted up on the day the game ends; as of [0.2.11](/wiki/Changelog#0.2.11 "Changelog") that's the **first day** of Y**2011** for both [campaigns](/wiki/Competing_CPUs "Competing CPUs"). You will get notified the game is about to end exactly 1 year before that happens.

## Online leaderboards\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fhardware-tycoon.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FHigh_score%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D4&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")\]

Since [0.2.11](/wiki/Changelog#0.2.11 "Changelog") it is possible to submit your score to the [online leaderboard](https://ht.poggers.ltd/scores).

Simply click the submit button that rolls down while the final score is counted, input your name, and click send.
