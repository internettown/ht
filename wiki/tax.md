Tax notification as of 0.2.6. Note that the text is outdated since tax is now calculated based on income.

**Tax** is paid based on last year's income and only if it was at least $1,000. By default it is done on the first day of every year, but it is possible to [pay it manually](/wiki/Bank#Taxes "Bank") up to 3 months later.

## Tax Rates\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fhardware-tycoon.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FTax%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")\]

The tax rate depends on the [difficulty](/wiki/Game_Settings#Difficulty_Settings "Game Settings") you're playing on:
